Healthcare Facilities Map

Project Overview

This project was carried out as part of an 18-day survey camp conducted by Group E. The primary objective was to perform an in-field, door-to-door survey focused on identifying and mapping healthcare facilities (Hospitals, Clinics, and Pharmacies) in the Biruta-17,Pokhara. The survey was conducted over two days, and data was collected directly from the field through personal interactions with facility representatives.

The data obtained has been analyzed and visualized using thematic maps and graphs shown below. The information displayed on the interactive map is a direct result of the field survey efforts.
